What Makes Us Special

Discover why our customers love dining at Nate Café

Farm to Table

We source the freshest local ingredients daily from trusted Ethiopian farms to ensure every dish bursts with authentic, natural flavor.

Artisan Coffee

Expertly brewed Ethiopian coffee, from classic macchiatos to specialty lattes. Every cup is a celebration of Ethiopia's rich coffee heritage.

Signature Mocktails

Refreshing, handcrafted alcohol-free cocktails that elevate any meal. Our 9 signature mocktails are made with fresh fruits and premium ingredients.

Artisan Pizza

Wood-fired pizzas with premium toppings — from classic Margareta to our famous Nate All Season loaded with 5 toppings.

Event Hosting

Birthdays, anniversaries, corporate gatherings — our team will tailor the perfect experience for your special moments.

Fast Delivery

Enjoy Nate Café from the comfort of your home. Fast and reliable delivery across Addis Ababa, because great food should be accessible.

Free WiFi

Stay connected while you dine. Perfect for working professionals, students, or anyone who wants to enjoy great food with connectivity.

Parking Available

Convenient parking for all our guests. No stress, no hassle — just great food and good times.

Made with Love

Every dish, every drink, every moment at Nate is infused with care, passion, and genuine Ethiopian hospitality.
