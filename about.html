Premium Dining in Addis Ababa

Welcome to Nate Café & Restaurant, where culinary artistry meets Ethiopian hospitality. Founded with a passion for great food and warm service, we've become a beloved destination for locals and visitors alike.

We bring together the finest local ingredients and international techniques to create dishes that delight the senses. From sunrise breakfasts to evening mocktails, every moment at Nate is crafted with care.

Our chefs combine traditional Ethiopian flavors with global cuisine to offer a menu that's both familiar and exciting — always fresh, always delicious.
